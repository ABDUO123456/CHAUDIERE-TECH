ERROR DATABASE

E01
Lockout
Ignition failure

The system failed to detect a flame after several attempts.

Check gas supply, electrodes, and gas valve ionization.
E02
Safety
Safety Thermostat Intervention

The boiler reached the maximum temperature limit (Overheat).

Check pump operation and primary heat exchanger blockage.
E03
Exhaust
Pressure Switch Fault

Interruption in the smoke evacuation system or fan failure.

Inspect exhaust fan, venturi tubes, and flue pipes.
E04
Water
Low Water Pressure

Circuit pressure dropped below the minimum operational level (1.0 bar).

Refill the system and check for hydraulic leaks.
E05
Sensor
Heating NTC Sensor

The heating delivery sensor is shorted or disconnected.

Check wiring or replace the delivery sensor.
E06
Sensor
Domestic Hot Water (DHW) Sensor

DHW NTC sensor fault detected during hot water demand.

Replace DHW sensor or check cable integrity.
E12
Internal
Delivery Sensor Anomaly

Incorrect reading from the primary circuit delivery sensor.

Inspect PCB connection to heating sensor.
E31
Comm
Remote Control Error

Loss of communication between the boiler and the remote unit.

Verify OpenTherm wiring and remote controller power.
E41
Service
Solar/Pre-heat Sensor

Fault in the DHW pre-heating or solar integration sensor.

Check solar circuit sensor or external tank NTC.
E72
Combustion
Burner Control Error

The system detected poor combustion quality or signal loss.

Clean burner and calibrate gas valve CO2 levels.
E76
Valve
Modulator Error

The gas valve modulation coil is disconnected or faulty.

Check solenoid resistance on the gas valve.
E99
System
General Logic Error

Internal PCB software or hardware malfunction.

Perform a system Reset; if it persists, replace the PCB.
